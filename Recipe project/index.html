White Sauce Pasta

Recipe taken from google

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (100 grams) pasta
  • 1½ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter + ½ tablespoon oil or butter
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • 1½ tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves grated
  • ½ teaspoon oregano
  • ½ teaspoon red chili flakes

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil or butter and sauté grated garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add butter and let it melt. Then add flour and cook for a minute to form a roux.
  4. Gradually whisk in milk, ensuring no lumps form. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add heavy cream, black pepper, oregano, and red chili flakes. Mix well.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs or grated cheese if desired.
